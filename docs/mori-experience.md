# Mori Experience

Mori is Wanderboard’s AI travel companion, but its role changes depending on what the traveller is doing.

## Plan and Discover

In Plan and Discover, Mori helps turn broad travel ideas into concrete possibilities.

Mori can suggest:

- Attractions and landmarks
- Food stops and dining areas
- Neighbourhoods
- Cultural activities
- Nature spots
- Shopping areas
- Quieter alternatives
- Places that match the traveller’s interests and existing route

Suggestions are returned as reviewable place cards rather than being saved automatically.

When coordinates are available, suggested places can also appear as temporary markers on the map. The traveller can inspect, save, assign, or dismiss each suggestion.

## Day Itinerary

In the Day Itinerary view, Mori focuses on whether a plan is practical.

Mori can:

- Reorder places into a more sensible route
- Move activities between days
- Reduce unnecessary travel
- Add food or rest breaks
- Identify overly busy days
- Flag uncertain opening-time compatibility
- Suggest nearby replacements
- Explain the trade-offs behind a proposed change

Changes are presented as structured itinerary proposals with an explanation and, where practical, a before-and-after preview.

The itinerary changes only after the traveller approves the proposal.

Opening hours are never presented as current or verified unless supported by a reliable source. Otherwise, Mori identifies the information as uncertain and recommends checking the official listing.

## Guide Mode

During the trip, Mori becomes a more conversational companion.

Guide Mode uses the current day, remaining plans, nearby saved places, and available grounded destination knowledge to answer questions such as:

- What should I do next?
- What still fits today?
- Is there somewhere nearby to eat?
- Which activity can I skip?
- How can I make the rest of the day less rushed?
- What is a good alternative if plans change?

Guide Mode is designed to feel calm, warm, practical, and gently playful. It gives the traveller a useful next step without overwhelming them or rebuilding the whole itinerary.

## Structured Product Actions

Across all three experiences, Mori’s conversational reply remains separate from application actions.

A response can include:

- A traveller-friendly message
- Suggested places
- Temporary map suggestions
- Itinerary proposals
- Guide actions
- Warnings
- Grounding status
- Supporting sources

The visible message does not expose internal place IDs, day IDs, mutation names, tool calls, schemas, raw JSON, or hidden reasoning.

AI-generated suggestions remain temporary until the traveller chooses to save or apply them.
